Spider-Man

Identity: Miles Morales
From: Brooklyn, NY
Created: August 3, 2011

Spider-Man, created by Brian Michael Bendis and Sara Pichelli in 2011, is the alter ego of Miles Morales, a typically 13 year old from Brooklyn who also got his powers from a radioactive spider, giving him the same abilities as Spider-Man with the adition of two powers which is a bio-electric charge/electrical powers, and invisibility at times. His story differs like in some where him and Peter team up or traveling the Spider-Verse, but his main story is taking the mantle of Spider-Man after the death of Peter Parker and continuing to protect the city. Miles is still somewhat recent being created in 2011 but he is no doubt the most popular Spider-Man variant and a fan favorite.

Popular Media

  • Ultimate Spider-Man (2012), his first appearance outside the comics was in this show, he appeared in a series of episodes related to the Spider-Verse in 2016
  • Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse (2018), Miles Morales got his own movie in December 2018 which was animated and was extremely loved by everyone, he then got a second movie in 2022, and I believe one last movie but the release date is unknown because of delays.
  • Insomiac Games, Miles appeared in the 2017 Spider-Man PlayStation game and recieved his powers at the end of the game teasing to his own game which came out in 2020, then the second Spider-Man game came out in 2023 which was a teamup between the two heroes.

Most famous quote

"It's a leap of faith, Miles. That's all it is." said by Peter B. Parker in the 2018 Spider-Verse film when he tried to encourage Miles to risk and try to reach his abilities since for most of the movie, he was struggling to use his powers.
