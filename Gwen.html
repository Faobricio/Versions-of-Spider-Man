Spider-Gwen

Identity: Gwen Stacey
From: Chelsea, NY
Created: December, 1965

Spider-Gwen, who also commonly goes by "Ghost-Spider" to hide her identity, Gwen Stacey was created in 1965 by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko but her "Spider-Gwen" character was created in 2014 by Jason Latour and Robbi Rodriguez. Gwen Stacey, a 16-17 year old from Chelsea who as well got her powers from a radioactive spider bite, giving her the same powers as Spider-Man. She is from Earth-65 as Miles is from Earth-1610, and the main Spider-Man is from Earth-161, in her universe, Peter Parker is her best friend, he wanted to be speicial like Spider-Gwen so he experimented with himself and turned into the lizard but lost control, so Gwen accidentally killed him in their fight while she didn't know that was Peter, blaming herself for his death when she did find out. She's arguably the third most popular Spider-Man variant appearing in most of the same media as Miles Morales.

Popular Media

  • Ultimate Spider-Man (2012), her first appearance outside the comics was also around the same time as Miles, appearing in Spider-Verse related episodes in 2016
  • Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse (2018), Gwen Stacey appears in both the Spider-Verse movies, and will be in the third movie, plays one of the main characters who has her own problems with her dad and the death of her bestfriend, she helps Miles and is part of the Spider-Society.
  • Spider-Gwen is definitely a very popular character but she doesn't show up in as many things as Miles Morales, she's in a few other Spider-Man shows and very few Marvel games but she is rumored to possibly show up in the next Insomniac Spider-Man game and will show up in the next season of "Your Friendly Neightborhood Spider-Man" show on Disney+.
